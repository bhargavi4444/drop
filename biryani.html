Hyderabadi Biryani

INGREDIENTS
  • Chikan
  • Basmati rice
  • Spices
  • garlic
  • Mint
  • Mint leaf
  • onions
  • yoghurt
  • salt
  • ghee
  • Cinnamon sticks
  1. Clean the meat. Now in a pan, add meat, salt, ginger garlic paste, red chilli powder, green chilli paste,
    sauteed brown onions, cardamom powder, cinnamon, cumin seeds, cloves, mace, mint leaves and lemon juice.
  2. Mix all of these ingredients thoroughly together.
  3. Now apply sticky dough on the sides of the pan.
  4. Cover it with a lid and cook for about 25 minutes.
  5. Hyderabadi biryani is ready to eat. Garnish it with boiled eggs, sliced carrots, cucumbers and serve hot.
